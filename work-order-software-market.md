<p><p>Work order software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, enabling access from any device with internet connectivity, promoting collaboration and scalability. This model typically involves subscription pricing and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing more control over data and customization. However, it requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for organizations with specific compliance or security needs.</p></p>
