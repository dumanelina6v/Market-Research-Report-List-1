<p><p>Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) films are primarily categorized into two types: PVA Optical Film and PVA Water Soluble Film. PVA Optical Film is used mainly in electronic displays for enhancing brightness and contrast, providing high optical clarity. In contrast, PVA Water Soluble Film is designed for applications requiring quick dissolution in water, such as packaging for detergents or agricultural products. Both types leverage PVA's unique properties, including biodegradability and chemical resistance, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
