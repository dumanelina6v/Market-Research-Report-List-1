<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) systems utilize fiber optic cables to measure temperature over extensive distances. The market is divided into two primary types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode DTS uses a single light path, enabling higher sensitivity and longer distance measurements, ideal for applications like oil and gas pipelines. In contrast, Multi-Mode DTS employs multiple light paths, offering lower sensitivity but a more cost-effective solution for shorter distances, such as in HVAC systems or building monitoring.</p></p>
