<p><strong>The Radar Level Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact Radar Level Gauge</li><li>Guided Wave Radar Level Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radar Level Gauge market comprises two primary types: Non-contact Radar Level Gauges and Guided Wave Radar Level Gauges. Non-contact Radar Level Gauges utilize microwave radar signals to measure fluid levels from a distance, making them ideal for high-temperature and pressure applications without interference from vapors or dust. Conversely, Guided Wave Radar Level Gauges use a probe that transmits radar pulses along its length, suitable for more viscous or turbulent materials. Both types offer high accuracy and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
