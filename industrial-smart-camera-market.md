<p><strong>The Industrial Smart Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Smart Camera Market is primarily divided into two types: Area Scan Cameras and Line Scan Cameras. Area Scan Cameras capture images in a rectangular frame, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed inspection of stationary objects, such as assembly lines. In contrast, Line Scan Cameras scan one line of pixels at a time, making them suitable for capturing images of moving objects or very long products, such as rolls of fabric or metal sheets, enabling high-speed processing and analysis.</p></p>
