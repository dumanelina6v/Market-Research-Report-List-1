<p><p>The lithium disilicate market is categorized into three main types: veneering, crowns, and bridges. Veneering involves thin layers of lithium disilicate used to enhance the aesthetic appeal of existing teeth or restorations. Crowns are full-coverage restorations that provide strength and durability to compromised teeth. Bridges replace missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth, using lithium disilicate for its aesthetic qualities and high strength. Each type caters to specific dental needs while maintaining a natural appearance and functionality.</p></p>
