<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Splitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber splitters market is classified mainly into Type I and Type II. Type I splitters, commonly known as fused biconical taper (FBT) splitters, are cost-effective and suitable for small-scale applications, offering low insertion loss. In contrast, Type II splitters, or planar lightwave circuits (PLC), are preferred for larger networks due to their ability to handle multiple channels and greater wavelength stability. Both types play a crucial role in distributing optical signals in telecommunications and data communication systems.</p></p>
