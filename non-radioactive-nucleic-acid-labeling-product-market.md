<p><p>The non-radioactive nucleic acid labeling product market is primarily categorized into several types, including Biotin, DIG System, Fluorescent, and Others. Biotin labeling utilizes streptavidin-biotin interactions for high sensitivity in detection. The DIG System involves chemical modifications that facilitate colorimetric or chemiluminescent detection. Fluorescent labeling employs various fluorescent dyes for real-time imaging and quantification in molecular biology applications. "Others" encompasses alternative labeling methods such as enzyme-based or peptide nucleic acid (PNA) approaches, catering to diverse research needs.</p></p>
