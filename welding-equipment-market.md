<p><ul><li>Arc Welding</li><li>Electrogas/Electroslag Welding</li><li>Friction Welding</li><li>Laser & Electron-Beam Welding</li><li>Resistance Welding</li><li>Oxy-Fuel Welding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding equipment market encompasses various types, each suited for specific applications. Arc welding uses electric arcs for fusing metals, while electrogas and electroslag welding involve molten metal pools for thicker workpieces. Friction welding generates heat through friction, and laser/electron-beam welding offers precision through focused energy beams. Resistance welding uses electric current to join metals at contact points. Oxy-fuel welding employs a flame from burning gas and oxygen for metalwork. Other methods include ultrasonic and submerged arc welding, expanding industry capabilities.</p></p>
