<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble</li><li>Alcohol Solubility</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soluble dye market is primarily categorized into two types: oil-soluble and alcohol-soluble dyes. Oil-soluble dyes are designed for applications involving fats, oils, and hydrocarbons, making them ideal for products like cosmetics and plastics. In contrast, alcohol-soluble dyes dissolve in alcohol-based solvents, making them suitable for applications in inks, coatings, and food coloring. Each type serves distinct industries, ensuring compatibility with specific formulations and enhancing color performance across various products.</p></p>
