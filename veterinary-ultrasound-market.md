<p><ul><li>2D Ultrasound</li><li>3D & 4D Ultrasound</li><li>Doppler Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary ultrasound market encompasses various types of imaging technologies. 2D ultrasound provides two-dimensional images of internal structures, commonly used for basic diagnostics. 3D ultrasound offers three-dimensional images, enhancing visualization of organs and tissues for more detailed assessments. 4D ultrasound adds a time component, allowing for real-time visualization of moving structures, beneficial for monitoring fetal development. Doppler ultrasound evaluates blood flow and circulation, aiding in the diagnosis of heart and vascular conditions in animals.</p></p>
