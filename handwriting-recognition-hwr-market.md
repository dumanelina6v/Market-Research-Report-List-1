<p><p>Handwriting Recognition (HWR) refers to the technology that converts handwritten text into digital format. The market can be categorized into offline and online handwriting recognition. Offline handwriting recognition involves the analysis of scanned documents or images of handwritten text, allowing for batch processing without the need for immediate interaction. In contrast, online handwriting recognition captures handwriting in real-time via a stylus or touch screen, enabling immediate feedback and interaction, which is often used in applications like note-taking and signature verification.</p></p>
