<p><p>Segmented ball valves are classified into two main types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves feature a resilient seat, typically made from materials like rubber, providing a tight seal and reducing leakage, making them ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, hard seal valves use rigid seat materials such as metal or ceramic, offering enhanced durability and resistance to high temperatures and pressures, suitable for more demanding industrial conditions. Both types serve distinct operational needs within various industries.</p></p>
