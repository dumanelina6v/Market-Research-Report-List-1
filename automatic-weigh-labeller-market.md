<p><p>The Automatic Weigh Labeller market consists of two primary types: semi-automatic and fully automatic weigh labellers. Semi-automatic weigh labellers require manual intervention for loading products, making them suitable for smaller operations or less complex packaging lines. In contrast, fully automatic weigh labellers integrate advanced technology for seamless operation, automatically handling product weighing, labeling, and packaging without human input. This makes them ideal for high-volume production environments where efficiency and precision are critical, driving increased automation in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
