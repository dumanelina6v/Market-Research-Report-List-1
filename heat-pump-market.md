<p><p>The heat pump market can be categorized primarily into air-to-air and air-to-water systems. Air-to-air heat pumps transfer heat between the air inside a building and the outside air, primarily for space heating and cooling. In contrast, air-to-water heat pumps extract heat from the air to provide hot water for radiators or underfloor heating systems. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to different needs in residential and commercial sectors, while promoting energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
