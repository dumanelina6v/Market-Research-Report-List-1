<p><strong>The Sodium Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sodium ion selective electrodes (ISEs) market features two primary types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a solvent-based membrane that selectively allows sodium ions to pass through, providing high sensitivity and response speed. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride as the matrix, offering durability and cost-effectiveness while still effectively measuring sodium concentration. Both types serve critical roles in applications such as environmental monitoring, food safety, and clinical diagnostics, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
