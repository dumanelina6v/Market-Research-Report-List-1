<p><p>The hydraulic forklift scale market is divided into two primary types: wired forklift scales and wireless forklift scales. Wired forklift scales are connected to the forklift through cables, offering real-time weight measurements but may limit mobility due to their tethered design. Conversely, wireless forklift scales utilize wireless technology to transmit weight data, allowing for greater freedom of movement and flexibility in operations. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling, enabling accurate weight measurements during lifting and transporting tasks.</p></p>
