<p><p>The surveillance camera lens market is categorized into three main types: fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses have a set focal length, offering a stable field of view ideal for specific monitoring needs. Varifocal lenses allow for adjustable focal length, enabling users to modify the field of view without changing the lens, providing flexibility. Zoom lenses offer the ability to vary focal length through an optical mechanism, allowing for dynamic adjustments to capture distant subjects effectively, enhancing surveillance capabilities.</p></p>
