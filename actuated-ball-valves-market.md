<p><p>The actuated ball valves market includes various types based on their actuation method. Electrically actuated ball valves utilize electric motors for precise control, making them suitable for automated systems. Pneumatically actuated ball valves rely on compressed air for operation, offering fast action and responsiveness, ideal for larger applications. Air actuated ball valves operate similarly but focus on quick on-off control with minimal power requirements. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and reliability in fluid control systems.</p></p>
