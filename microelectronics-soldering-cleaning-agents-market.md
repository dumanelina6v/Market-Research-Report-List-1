<p><p>The microelectronics soldering cleaning agents market comprises primarily two types: water-based and semi-aqueous cleaning agents. Water-based cleaning agents utilize water as the main solvent, effectively removing flux residues and contaminants in a more environmentally friendly manner. Semi-aqueous cleaning agents, on the other hand, combine both water and organic solvents, offering enhanced cleaning power for challenging residues while still being less hazardous than traditional solvents. Both types aim to ensure high reliability and performance of electronic components.</p></p>
