<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) system market comprises several key components: </p><p>1. **Hard Tags** are durable devices used on merchandise to prevent theft, typically deactivated or removed at the point of sale.</p><p>2. **Soft Tags** are often made of adhesive materials and are more discreet, suitable for tagging smaller or more delicate items.</p><p>3. **Detachers** are tools used to remove hard tags from products.</p><p>4. **Detection Systems** consist of antennas or sensors installed at store exits, triggering alarms if tagged items pass through without proper deactivation.</p></p>
