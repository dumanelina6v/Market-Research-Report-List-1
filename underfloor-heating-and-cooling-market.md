<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating and Cooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric System</li><li>Hydronic System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating and cooling systems provide temperature regulation through two main types: electric and hydronic. The electric system uses electric cables or mats installed beneath the flooring, offering easy installation and quick heat response, making it ideal for smaller spaces or retrofitting. Conversely, the hydronic system circulates heated water through pipes under the floor, providing efficient and even warmth, perfect for larger areas and new builds. Both systems enhance comfort and energy efficiency in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
