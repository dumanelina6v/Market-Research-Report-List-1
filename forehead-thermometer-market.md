<p><p>The forehead thermometer market is divided into two primary types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact thermometers measure temperature from a distance, using infrared technology to provide quick readings without physical contact. They are widely used for convenience and hygiene, especially in settings like schools and hospitals. In contrast, contact thermometers require direct skin contact for accurate readings. They generally offer more precise measurements but may be less convenient and hygienic, making them suitable for home healthcare scenarios.</p></p>
