<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Smart Motorway Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controlled Motorways</li><li>Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running Schemes</li><li>All Lane Running Schemes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Motorway market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Controlled Motorways** regulate traffic with variable speed limits and lane restrictions based on real-time conditions, enhancing flow and safety. </p><p>2. **Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running Schemes** convert the hard shoulder into an active lane during peak times, increasing capacity while maintaining emergency access. </p><p>3. **All Lane Running Schemes** eliminate the hard shoulder entirely, allowing all lanes to serve traffic, which optimizes road use and minimizes congestion while ensuring safety through technology-driven management.</p></p>
