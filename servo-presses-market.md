<p><strong>The Servo Presses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crank Servo Presses</li><li>Screw Servo Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The servo presses market includes two primary types: crank servo presses and screw servo presses. Crank servo presses utilize a rotating crank mechanism to convert rotary motion into linear force, providing high precision and speed for metal forming applications. Screw servo presses, on the other hand, employ a screw mechanism to apply force, allowing for fine control over stroke length and speed, ideal for delicate tasks. Both types offer energy efficiency, reduced noise, and enhanced process control in manufacturing.</p></p>
