<p><p>Plant growth test chambers come in two primary types: reach-in and walk-in chambers. Reach-in chambers are compact, designed for small spaces, and allow easy access to plants for research and experimentation. These units are ideal for laboratories with limited room. In contrast, walk-in chambers are larger, accommodating extensive research with multiple plant species, providing more space for experimentation. They are suitable for institutions requiring substantial growth area while maintaining controlled environmental conditions for optimal plant development.</p></p>
