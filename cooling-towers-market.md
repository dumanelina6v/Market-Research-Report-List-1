<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit Cooling Towers</li><li>Closed-Circuit Cooling Towers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooling towers are used to dissipate heat from industrial processes or HVAC systems. Open-circuit cooling towers operate by drawing in outside air to cool water through evaporation, making them efficient for large applications. In contrast, closed-circuit cooling towers use a closed-loop system where water circulates through a heat exchanger, minimizing water loss and contamination while providing efficient cooling in compact designs. Both types cater to different operational needs and resource management strategies within various industries.</p></p>
