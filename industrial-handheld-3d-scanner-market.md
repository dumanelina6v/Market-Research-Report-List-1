<p><strong>The Industrial Handheld 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structure Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial handheld 3D scanner market primarily includes two main types: laser scanners and structured light scanners. Laser scanners use a focused laser beam to capture precise surface details, making them ideal for complex geometries and large objects. In contrast, structured light scanners project a series of light patterns onto the object, capturing detailed surface information through camera systems. Both technologies enable high-resolution 3D modeling, with laser scanners excelling in range and accuracy, while structured light scanners offer faster scanning times and detailed capture.</p></p>
