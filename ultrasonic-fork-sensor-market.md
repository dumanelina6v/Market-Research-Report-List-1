<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Fork Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NPN</li><li>PNP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Fork Sensor market is categorized into two main types based on output configuration: NPN and PNP sensors. NPN sensors provide a negative output, commonly used in applications requiring a common ground, while PNP sensors offer a positive output, suitable for applications with a positive voltage supply. Both types detect objects by emitting ultrasonic waves, providing reliable measurements and positioning in various industrial settings. Their distinct output types cater to different electrical systems and user preferences, enhancing operational flexibility.</p></p>
