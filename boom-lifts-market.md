<p><p>The boom lifts market encompasses various types designed for different applications. Telescopic boom lifts feature an extendable arm for reaching great heights and long distances, making them ideal for construction. Articulating boom lifts have jointed arms that allow for maneuverability around obstacles. Trailer-mounted boom lifts provide portability for jobs requiring mobility. Bucket trucks are specialized for reaching overhead work areas, often used in utility services. Towable boom lifts combine portability with versatility, suitable for rental and lighter tasks.</p></p>
