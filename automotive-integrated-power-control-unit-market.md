Engine Power IPCUs manage the distribution and control of energy within internal combustion engines, optimizing performance and efficiency. Motor Power IPCUs focus on electric and hybrid vehicle applications, regulating power delivery to electric motors for improved acceleration and energy management. Electric Power IPCUs oversee the vehicle's electrical systems, ensuring proper energy distribution for various components and enhancing overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
