<p><ul><li>PA</li><li>PEI</li><li>PEEK and PEKK</li><li>Reinforced HPPs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D printing high-performance plastic market encompasses several key types, including PA (polyamide), PEI (polyetherimide), PEEK (polyether ether ketone), and PEKK (polyether ketone ketone). PA is known for its strength and flexibility, while PEI offers exceptional heat resistance and mechanical properties. PEEK stands out for its thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications. PEKK combines features of both PEEK and polyimides. Additionally, reinforced high-performance plastics (HPPs) incorporate fibers or fillers to enhance strength, heat resistance, and overall performance.</p></p>
