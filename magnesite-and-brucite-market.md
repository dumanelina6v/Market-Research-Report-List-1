<p><strong>The Magnesite and Brucite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phanerocrystalline magnesite</li><li>Cryptocrystalline magnesite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesite and brucite markets consist of distinct types based on their crystalline structures. Phanerocrystalline magnesite features visible crystals, making it suitable for industrial applications like refractory materials and ceramics. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite has a fine-grained structure, which limits visibility but enhances certain properties, making it preferable for specialized uses, including magnesium production and fertilizers. Both types have unique market demands and applications, driven by their distinct characteristics and industry requirements. Brucite, as a magnesium hydroxide mineral, further complements these markets with its fire-retardant properties and environmental applications.</p></p>
