<p><strong>The CBCT Dental Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Image Intensifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CBCT dental imaging technology primarily utilizes two types of detectors: flat panel detectors and image intensifiers. Flat panel detectors offer high-resolution images with enhanced sensitivity and reduced radiation exposure, making them ideal for precise diagnostic imaging. On the other hand, image intensifiers amplify the incoming X-ray signal, providing visual images in real-time, traditionally used in fluoroscopic applications. Both technologies cater to different clinical needs, influencing the adoption and efficiency of CBCT systems in dental practices.</p></p>
