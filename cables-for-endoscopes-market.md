<p><p>The market for cables used in endoscopy consists of three main types: lighting cables, data cables, and hybrid cables. Lighting cables provide illumination essential for clear visibility during procedures, ensuring optimal image quality. Data cables facilitate the transmission of images and patient information between the endoscope and monitoring equipment, enhancing diagnostic capabilities. Hybrid cables combine both lighting and data functionalities into a single unit, streamlining connections and reducing clutter, making them increasingly popular in advanced medical applications.</p></p>
