<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) enhance the capacity and flexibility of power transmission networks. Shunt compensation involves adding reactive power to stabilize voltage levels and improve system efficiency by using devices like Static Var Compensators. Series compensation, on the other hand, injects reactive power into the system to increase transmission capacity and improve stability, often through Series Capacitors. Other market types include advanced technologies such as thyristor-controlled reactors and dynamic voltage restorers, which provide additional adaptability in power systems.</p></p>
